I’m Cheyenne. I write because therapy has a waitlist.

I’ve been abandoned, ghosted, gaslit, griefed, girlbossed, and spiritually jump-scared. So I made a blog.

This is where I scream into the internet instead of into my pillow. Where I call out the trauma, text my inner child back, and emotionally spiral in lowercase. If you’ve ever cried in a Hobby Lobby parking lot while whispering “it’s fine”… welcome home.

I write essays about healing, humor, God, grief, and glitter. Also: emotional damage, religious trauma, self-love with a side of delusion, and why I keep texting people who were clearly sent by Satan.

This site exists because I got tired of ghosting myself. So I made a little internet corner to sit in the wreckage and roast marshmallows. You don’t have to be okay to be here. You just have to be honest — or ready to be.
